Two-page poster abstracts
  • January 20, 2020
  • 10 Comments

To encourage discussion of late-breaking results or for work that is not sufficiently mature for a full paper, we welcome 2-page abstracts, with deadline April 14. This gives authors the opportunity to present their latest research results to the community, and is also an opportunity for researchers new to the field to participate in the conference. Two-page poster abstracts should present work within the conference scope that is original and new. These submissions will NOT be included in the IEEE Xplore conference proceedings. Accepted abstracts will be presented during poster sessions. To submit a contribution, follow the link under ICDL-EPIROB 2010 here: The format for these papers is the one described below, with a page limit of 2 pages, including references.

Paper formatting
  • January 30, 2020
  • 5 Comments

LaTeX and Word Templates for the conference papers are available. The templates are standard IEEE two-column paper templates (page size US letter), to be used for all submission types. We are employing a single-blind review process, so you are not required to anonymize your submission. The paper length limit for regular papers is 6 pages including references (up to 2 more pages are allowed for a fee of 1000 NOK per page). Submissions may be updated at any time until the paper deadline.
