About Event

Conference Venue:

PKU Overseas Exchange Center (https://oec.pku.edu.cn/index.htm), founded in 1993, is one of the windows of the international exchange of Peking University. With the star-level service, the center builds up a professional and efficient working team, to undertake foreign exchange in international conference as well as overseas visit reception. Over the past 20 years, the center has received more than 200,000 guests from more than 50 countries and regions, including dozens of Presidents or Prime Ministers.
The center has more than 10 meeting rooms with different capacities, which is suitable for the IEEE ICDL 2021 conference.

location

The location of the conference venue is near the East Gate and Southeast Gate of the campus, and marked in red in the campus map as above figures shown.
Also, we organize enough signages and a lot of student volunteers for assistance of the IEEE ICDL 2021 participants in this venue. WiFi is available at the conference venue during the whole conference dates.
