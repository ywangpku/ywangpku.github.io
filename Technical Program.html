Social Events

August 23rd, 2021 -- August 26th, 2021

We want to ensure that IEEE ICDL 2021 delegates will have the opportunity to experience Chinese culture during their time outside the scientific program. The social events are therefore planned in traditional yet modern environments or with a touch of Chinese culture for a most memorable evening for all guests.

The Welcome Reception will be held at the main venue, Peking University. The same space as the Poster Presentation and the Industry Exhibition will stage a perfect setting to start the networking among the Meeting attendees.

Chinese traditional food and drinks will be served.

  • First day with workshops and tutorials: August 23rd, 2021
  • Three days of oral and poster sessions: August 24th – 26th, 2021 3-6 keynote speakers
